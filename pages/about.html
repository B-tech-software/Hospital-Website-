Our Story of Healing and Hope

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the values that guide us.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, patient-centered healthcare to the Harare community through advanced technology, clinical excellence, and a deep-seated culture of compassion. We strive to be a beacon of health and wellness, accessible to all.

Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe's leading private healthcare institution, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and fostering a healthier future for generations to come.

Our Journey

2015 - The Foundation

The vision for Mount Pleasant Premier Hospital was born, with a groundbreaking ceremony marking the start of our commitment.

2018 - Doors Open

We officially opened our doors to the public, welcoming our first patients with a state-of-the-art facility and a team of 50 dedicated staff.

2021 - Cardiac Centre of Excellence

Launched our advanced Cardiology unit, performing the first successful open-heart surgery at our facility.

2025 - Community Health Initiative

Expanded our outreach with free community health screening programs across Harare, reaffirming our commitment to public wellness.
