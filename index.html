Welcome to Mount Pleasant Premier Hospital

Founded on the principles of compassion, innovation, and excellence, Mount Pleasant Premier Hospital is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to providing the highest standard of healthcare to our community.

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology with a patient-first approach, ensuring you and your loved ones receive personalized and effective treatment. We believe in health, hope, and healing for all.

Our Services

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care, from diagnosis to intervention, ensuring your heart is in the best hands.

Radiology & Imaging

State-of-the-art imaging technology for accurate diagnoses, including MRI, CT scans, and X-rays.

Maternity & Paediatrics

Comprehensive care for mothers and children in a safe and nurturing environment.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system by top specialists.

24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency department is always open and equipped to handle any medical crisis.

Pharmacy Services

On-site pharmacy providing convenient access to a wide range of medications.
